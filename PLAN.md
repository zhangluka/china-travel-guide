## Content Template (per city page)

Each city page should follow this structure for consistency:

1. **Hero** — City name, tagline, quick facts (population, budget, best time, English level)
2. **Why Visit** — 3 selling points in 1 sentence each
3. **Top Attractions** — Card grid with links
4. **Food Guide** — Must-eat dishes with restaurant names
5. **Getting There** — Train/flight from major cities with times
6. **Getting Around** — Metro map, taxi tips, walking areas
7. **Where to Stay** — Area recommendation by budget
8. **Itinerary** — 3-day template with time-of-day breakdown
9. **FAQ** — Schema-markup ready (5-8 questions)
10. **Related Guides** — Internal links to survival + other cities
