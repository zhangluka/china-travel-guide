# Maps & Navigation in China

**Priority: 🟡 Learn before your trip**

Google Maps has very limited functionality in China — navigation doesn't work, many locations are missing, and the data is outdated. You need a Chinese map app.

## The Problem with Google Maps in China

- ❌ Navigation (driving/walking directions) doesn't work
- ❌ Many businesses and POIs are missing
- ❌ No real-time traffic data
- ❌ Address search is unreliable
- ❌ Requires VPN to access

## Use Amap (Gaode Maps) Instead

**Amap (高德地图)** is China's most popular navigation app. It's like Google Maps but with much better data for China.

### How to Get It

- **iOS**: Search "Amap" or "高德地图" in the App Store
- **Android**: Download from the Amap website (Google Play is blocked in China)

### How to Use It (Even Though It's in Chinese)

The interface is primarily in Chinese, but you can still use it effectively:

#### Searching for Places

1. **Search in English**: Some popular tourist spots appear in English
2. **Search in Pinyin**: Type the pinyin of a place name (e.g., "Chunxi Road" for 春熙路)
3. **Copy-paste Chinese**: Copy the Chinese name from Google/TripAdvisor and paste into Amap
4. **Use coordinates**: If you have GPS coordinates, paste them directly

#### Navigation

1. Search for your destination
2. Tap the blue "路线" (Route) button
3. Choose: 🚗 Drive | 🚌 Transit | 🚶 Walk | 🚲 Bike
4. The app shows routes with time estimates
5. Tap "开始导航" (Start Navigation) to begin turn-by-turn directions

## Alternative: Maps.me

**Maps.me** is an offline map app that works in China:

- ✅ Works without internet (download maps in advance)
- ✅ English interface
- ✅ Good for hiking and outdoor activities
- ❌ POI data is less complete than Amap
- ❌ Navigation is less accurate
- ❌ Some hotels/restaurants are missing

> 💡 **Pro tip**: Use Maps.me for offline backup and hiking trails, but Amap for day-to-day navigation.

## How Chinese Addresses Work

Chinese addresses are written in the **opposite order** from English:

- **English**: Street → City → State → Country
- **Chinese**: Country → Province → City → District → Street → Building

Example:
- **English**: 88 Chunxi Road, Jinjiang District, Chengdu
- **Chinese**: 成都市锦江区春熙路88号

When entering an address in Amap, you usually just need the **street name and number** or the **landmark name**.

## Finding Your Way Back to Your Hotel

**Before you leave your hotel each morning:**

1. Open Amap
2. Tap on your hotel location
3. Tap "收藏" (Save/Bookmark) — the star icon
4. Now you can always navigate back by tapping the saved location

> 💡 **Pro tip**: Take a photo of your hotel's business card (usually at the front desk). It has the Chinese address, which you can show to taxi drivers or paste into Amap.

## Getting a Taxi Without Chinese

If you can't use Didi and need to hail a taxi:

1. Open Amap and find your destination
2. Show the map screen to the driver — they can see the location
3. Or take a screenshot of the Chinese address and show it to the driver
4. Use the "附近" (Nearby) feature to find your hotel's exact Chinese name
