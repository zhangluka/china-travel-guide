# 📅 Guilin 3-Day Itinerary

This itinerary covers the essential Guilin experience: the iconic Li River Cruise, cycling through Yangshuo's karst countryside, and the stunning Longji Rice Terraces.

## Day 1: Li River Cruise & Yangshuo

### Morning (8:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
🛶 **Li River Cruise**
- Pickup from hotel ~8:00 AM
- Board the boat at Zhujiang Wharf
- 4-5 hour cruise through karst mountains
- Highlights: Elephant Trunk Hill, Mural Hill, ¥20 banknote scene
- See our [Li River Cruise Guide](/city/guilin/li-river-cruise/)

### Afternoon (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
🚶 **Explore Yangshuo**
- Arrive in Yangshuo, check into hotel
- Walk **West Street** — restaurants, shops, atmosphere
- Rent a bike for tomorrow (arrange at your hotel or a shop)

### Evening (6:00 PM - 10:00 PM)
🍺 **West Street Nightlife**
- Dinner at a local restaurant — try **Beer Fish** (啤酒鱼), Yangshuo's signature dish
- Live music bars on West Street
- Optional: **Impression Liu Sanjie** outdoor show (¥198-688)

## Day 2: Yangshuo Cycling & Yulong River

### Morning (7:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
🚲 **Cycling the Countryside**
- Rent an e-bike (¥80-150/day)
- Ride the **Ten-Mile Gallery** (十里画廊) — a scenic road with karst peaks
- Stop at **Big Banyan Tree** (大榕树, 1,400 years old)
- Continue to **Moon Hill** (月亮山) — climb 800 steps for panoramic views

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
🛶 **Yulong River Bamboo Rafting**
- Lunch in the countryside (village restaurant)
- Bamboo raft ride on the Yulong River (1.5-2 hours, ¥200-320 per raft)
- Peaceful, scenic, and much quieter than the Li River
- Cycle back to Yangshuo through rice paddies

### Evening
🍜 **Dinner in Yangshuo**
- Try local dishes: Li River snails, stuffed tofu, bamboo rice
- Rest early for Day 3

## Day 3: Longji Rice Terraces

### Full Day (6:00 AM - 7:00 PM)
🌾 **Longji Rice Terraces**
- Hire a car from Yangshuo (or return to Guilin first) — ~3 hours to Longji
- Visit **Ping'an Village** — the most accessible terrace area
- Walk to "Seven Stars with Moon" viewpoint
- Lunch at a local guesthouse (try bamboo rice and mountain vegetables)
- Walk to "Nine Dragons and Five Tigers" viewpoint
- Return to Guilin by evening

See our [Longji Rice Terraces Guide](/city/guilin/longji-rice-terraces/) for details.

### Alternative Day 3: Stay in Yangshuo

If you prefer more time in Yangshuo:
- **Morning**: Rock climbing lesson at a local climbing school
- **Afternoon**: Rent a scooter and explore further afield
- **Evening**: Cooking class — learn to make Beer Fish and Guilin rice noodles

## Tips

- **Book Li River Cruise through your hotel** — easiest and often cheapest
- **Rent an e-bike, not a regular bike** — the Yangshuo hills are tiring on a regular bike
- **Start cycling early** — morning light is best for photos, and it's cooler
- **Longji is a long day trip** — consider staying overnight if you want sunrise views
- **Beer Fish is a must** — it's Yangshuo's signature dish and it's delicious
- **Bring sunscreen** — there's no shade on the river or cycling routes
