# 🕳️ Wulong Karst National Geology Park (武隆喀斯特)

Massive natural sinkholes, towering stone bridges, and the filming location for Transformers 4 — Wulong is one of the most spectacular day trips from Chongqing.

## Overview

The **Wulong Karst** (武隆喀斯特) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site about 2.5 hours from Chongqing. The main attractions are the **Three Natural Bridges** (天生三桥) — three enormous stone arches spanning a giant sinkhole valley — and the **Furong Cave** (芙蓉洞), one of China's most impressive limestone caves.

## How to Get There

### Option 1: Train + Bus (Recommended)
1. **High-speed train** from Chongqing North Station to Wulong Station (~2 hours, ¥65)
2. **Shuttle bus** from Wulong Station to the scenic area (~30 min, ¥10)
3. Return the same way

### Option 2: Tour Bus
- Direct buses from Chongqing Sigongli Bus Station (~3 hours, ¥75)
- Less flexible but no transfers needed

### Option 3: Guided Tour
- Many day tours available on Trip.com, Klook, or via your hotel
- Typically ¥300-500 including transport, tickets, and lunch
- Convenient but less flexible

## What to See

### Three Natural Bridges (天生三桥)
The main attraction. Three massive natural stone arches in a row:
- **Tianlong Bridge** (天龙桥) — 235m high, 150m wide
- **Qinglong Bridge** (青龙桥) — 281m high, the tallest
- **Heilong Bridge** (黑龙桥) — 223m high, 193m wide

You walk down into the valley and look up at the bridges. It's awe-inspiring. The valley floor has a small village and the **"Transformers 4" filming location** — a stone building used in the movie.

### Furong Cave (芙蓉洞)
One of China's three most beautiful caves:
- 2,700 meters of passages
- Spectacular stalactites and stalagmites
- "Sleeping Beauty" and "Fairy Palace" formations
- Allow 1.5-2 hours inside

### Longshuixia Fissure (龙水峡地缝)
A narrow gorge with waterfalls:
- 2km walking path along the cliff face
- Multiple waterfalls
- Usually combined with the Three Natural Bridges

## Pro Tips

1. **Start early** — take the 7:00 AM train from Chongqing to maximize your time at Wulong.

2. **Wear comfortable shoes** — you'll walk 5-10km on uneven terrain, including hundreds of stairs.

3. **Bring water and snacks** — food options at the scenic area are limited and overpriced.

4. **Go on a weekday** — weekends and holidays draw massive domestic tourist crowds.

5. **Bring a rain jacket** — the valley floor can be damp, and weather changes quickly.

6. **The Three Natural Bridges are the priority** — if time is limited, skip Furong Cave and focus on the bridges.

7. **There's an elevator down** — you don't have to walk all the way down into the valley. Take the glass elevator (included in ticket).

## FAQ

**Is Wulong Karst a day trip from Chongqing?**
Yes, it's a popular day trip. The high-speed train takes about 2 hours each way, and you need 3-4 hours at the scenic area. Total: 8-10 hours including travel.

**How much does Wulong Karst cost?**
The Three Natural Bridges ticket is ¥125, Furong Cave is ¥120, and a combo ticket is ¥175. Add ¥130 for round-trip train and ¥20 for the shuttle bus.

**Was Transformers filmed at Wulong?**
Yes, Transformers: Age of Extinction (2014) filmed scenes at the Three Natural Bridges. There's a small "Transformers exhibition" at the bottom of the valley.

**Is Wulong Karst worth visiting?**
Absolutely. The scale of the natural bridges is breathtaking and unlike anything you'll see elsewhere. It's one of the best day trips from Chongqing.

**How do I get to Wulong from Chongqing?**
Take a high-speed train from Chongqing North Station to Wulong Station (~2 hours, ¥65), then a shuttle bus to the scenic area (~30 min, ¥10).
