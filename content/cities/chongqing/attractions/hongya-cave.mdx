# 🏮 Hongya Cave (洪崖洞)

Hongya Cave is Chongqing's most iconic landmark — a massive 11-story stilted building complex that looks like a scene from Spirited Away when lit up at night.

## Overview

**Hongyadong** (洪崖洞) is a recreation of the ancient stilted buildings (吊脚楼) that once lined the cliffs of the Jialing River. The current structure is a commercial complex with restaurants, shops, and observation decks, but the real magic happens after dark when thousands of golden lights transform it into a fairy-tale scene.

## How to Get There

- **Metro**: Line 1 or 6 to Xiaoshimen (小什字站), Exit 6, then walk 10 minutes
- **Didi/Taxi**: Show the driver "洪崖洞" (Hongyadong)
- **Walking**: 10 minutes from Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street

## What to See

### The Exterior (Free)
The best view is from the **opposite bank of the Jialing River** or from the **Qiansimen Bridge** (千厮门大桥). This is where all the viral photos are taken — the entire cliff-face building lit up in gold against the night sky.

### Inside the Complex
- **Floors 1-4**: Restaurants and food stalls (touristy, but convenient)
- **Floors 5-8**: Bars, cafes, and souvenir shops
- **Floor 11**: Observation deck with river views
- **The alleyways**: Narrow corridors between floors — explore them for atmospheric photos

### The Street Level
Enter from the **top** (Jiefangbei side) and walk down through the building. You'll exit at river level — this is the best way to experience the full height.

## Pro Tips

1. **Arrive at 6:00 PM** — the lights turn on around 6:30 PM (varies by season). Watching them illuminate is magical.

2. **Go to the bridge for photos** — Qiansimen Bridge (千厮门大桥) gives you the classic full-view shot. Walk to the middle of the pedestrian walkway.

3. **Skip the restaurants inside** — they're overpriced and mediocre. Eat elsewhere, come here for the views.

4. **Weekday evenings are less crowded** — weekends and holidays are packed shoulder-to-shoulder.

5. **Bring a jacket in winter** — the river wind is cold at night.

6. **Combine with Jiefangbei** — the pedestrian street is a 10-minute walk away and has much better food options.

## FAQ

**What is Hongya Cave?**
Hongya Cave (洪崖洞) is an 11-story stilted building complex on the cliff of the Jialing River in Chongqing. It's a recreation of traditional architecture and becomes a spectacular light show at night.

**Is Hongya Cave free to enter?**
Yes, entry is completely free. You can walk through all floors and enjoy the views without paying anything.

**What time do the lights turn on at Hongya Cave?**
The lights typically turn on around 6:30 PM, but the exact time varies by season (earlier in winter, later in summer). Arrive by 6:00 PM to secure a good viewing spot.

**Where is the best photo spot for Hongya Cave?**
The classic shot is from the middle of Qiansimen Bridge (千厮门大桥), which has a pedestrian walkway. This gives you the full building lit up with the river in the foreground.

**Is Hongya Cave like Spirited Away?**
The comparison to Studio Ghibli's Spirited Away is popular on social media. The golden-lit cliff buildings do resemble the film's bathhouse, though the creators have denied any connection.

**How long should I spend at Hongya Cave?**
1-2 hours is sufficient. Arrive before the lights turn on, walk through the building, take photos from the bridge, and leave.
