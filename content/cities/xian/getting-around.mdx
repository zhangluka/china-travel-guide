# 🚇 Getting Around Xi'an

Xi'an's old city is flat and compact — much easier to navigate than hilly cities like Chongqing. Most tourist attractions are within walking distance of each other.

## Metro (Recommended)

Xi'an's metro system has **8+ lines** covering most tourist areas.

### Essential Info

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Hours** | ~6:00 AM - 11:00 PM |
| **Cost** | ¥2-8 per trip (based on distance) |
| **Payment** | Alipay QR code, WeChat QR code, or buy single-use tokens |
| **Signs** | Chinese + English (station names and announcements) |

### Key Lines for Tourists

| Line | Key Stops |
|------|-----------|
| **Line 1** | Fangzhicheng (纺织城), Laodong Road (劳动路) |
| **Line 2** | Bell Tower (钟楼), Anyuanmen (安远门, North Gate), Yongningmen (永宁门, South Gate) |
| **Line 3** | Big Wild Goose Pagoda (大雁塔), Xiaozhai (小寨) |
| **Line 4** | North Station (北站), Dayanta (大雁塔) |
| **Line 14** | Airport (机场) |

### How to Use
1. **Buy a ticket**: Use the ticket machines (English interface available)
2. **Or use Alipay QR**: Open Alipay → search "西安地铁" → generate QR code
3. Find your platform (lines are color-coded)
4. Scan QR or insert token at exit

## Didi (Ride-Hailing)

**Didi** works well in Xi'an and has an English interface.

### Costs
- **Short trip (within city center)**: ¥10-20
- **Medium trip (across city)**: ¥20-40
- **To Terracotta Warriors**: ¥120-150 (consider Tour Bus 306 instead)
- **To/from airport**: ¥120-150

### Tips
- Xi'an's grid layout makes navigation easy — GPS works well here
- Most Didi drivers don't speak English
- Show your phone screen to the driver if needed

## Walking 🚶

Xi'an's old city center is very walkable. The city wall encloses the main tourist area.

**Walkable routes:**
- **Bell Tower to Muslim Quarter**: 5 minutes
- **Muslim Quarter to Drum Tower**: 2 minutes
- **Bell Tower to South Gate**: 15 minutes
- **South Gate to Big Wild Goose Pagoda**: 30 minutes

**Recommendation**: Walk within the old city walls. Use Didi/metro for attractions outside (Terracotta Warriors, Huashan).

## Bus 🚌

Buses are cheap (¥2) but confusing for non-Chinese speakers. Route names and stops are in Chinese only.

**Recommendation**: Skip the bus unless you're on a very tight budget. Metro + walking + Didi covers everything.

## Bike Rental 🚲

Xi'an is flat and bike-friendly. Great for:
- Biking the city wall (rent at the wall, ¥45/2 hours)
- Exploring the old city streets

Shared bikes (Meituan, Hello) are available via apps but require Chinese phone number and payment.

## From the Airport

### Xi'an Xianyang International Airport (XIY)
- **Metro Line 14**: ~40 min to Beikezhan (North Station), then transfer to city center. ¥9.
- **Airport bus**: Multiple routes to city, ¥25
- **Didi/Taxi**: ¥120-150, ~45-60 min

> 💡 **Pro tip**: Take the metro from the airport. It's cheap and fast. Transfer at Beikezhan to Line 2 or 4 for the city center.

## Pro Tips

1. **The city is flat** — unlike most Chinese cities, Xi'an is easy to walk. Wear comfortable shoes and explore on foot.

2. **The Bell Tower is the center** — all distances in Xi'an are measured from here. It's also the metro transfer hub.

3. **Get a transit QR code once** — set up Alipay's Xi'an metro QR code once, and use it for all metro trips.

4. **Walk inside the walls, Didi outside** — the old city (inside the wall) is walkable. For Terracotta Warriors, Huashan, or the airport, use buses or Didi.

5. **The South Gate area has the best nightlife** — bars, restaurants, and live music. Walkable from the Bell Tower.
