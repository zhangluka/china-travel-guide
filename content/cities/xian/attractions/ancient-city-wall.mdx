# 🏯 Xi'an Ancient City Wall (西安城墙)

The most complete ancient city wall in China — 14km of 600-year-old fortifications you can bike on, walk on, and watch the sunset from.

## Overview

Xi'an's **Ancient City Wall** (西安城墙) was built in 1370 during the Ming Dynasty. It's 13.7km long, 12-14m high, and 15-18m wide at the top. It's the most complete and best-preserved ancient city wall in China, and one of the largest military defense systems in the world.

## How to Get There

The wall has multiple gates with entry points:

| Gate | Metro Access | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **South Gate (永宁门)** | Line 2, Yongningmen Station | Most popular, best sunset views |
| **East Gate (长乐门)** | Line 4, Dachaishi Station | Less crowded |
| **North Gate (安远门)** | Line 2, Anyuanmen Station | Quiet, good photos |
| **West Gate (安定门)** | Bus access | Least crowded |

## What to Do

### Bike the Wall (Recommended) 🚲
The best way to experience the wall. Rent a bike and ride the full 14km loop.

- **Single bike**: ¥45 for 2 hours (deposit ¥200)
- **Tandem bike**: ¥90 for 2 hours (deposit ¥200)
- **Electric cart**: ¥80 for a section

Rental stations are at each gate. The surface is flat and smooth. Allow 1.5-2 hours for the full loop at a relaxed pace.

### Walk the Wall 🚶
If biking isn't your thing, walk a section. South Gate to East Gate is the most scenic stretch (~3km, 45 minutes).

### Watch the Sunset 🌅
The South Gate is the best spot for sunset. Arrive 1 hour before sunset, walk along the wall, and watch the city light up.

### Watch the Gate Ceremony
At the South Gate, there's a nightly gate-opening ceremony (usually 8:00 PM). It's a theatrical performance with Tang Dynasty costumes and music. Check times locally.

## Pro Tips

1. **Bike the full loop** — it's the quintessential Xi'an experience. The wall is flat and wide, perfect for casual cycling.

2. **Go late afternoon** — the light is beautiful for photos, and you can catch sunset. The wall is less crowded than morning.

3. **Start at the South Gate** — it's the most photogenic and has the best facilities (bike rental, restaurants nearby).

4. **Wear sunscreen** — there's no shade on the wall. In summer, it's brutal.

5. **Bring water** — there are limited vendors on the wall.

6. **The wall is lit at night** — if you stay past dark, the illuminated gates are beautiful from ground level.

## FAQ

**How long does it take to bike the Xi'an City Wall?**
About 1.5-2 hours at a relaxed pace. The wall is 13.7km around and the surface is flat and smooth.

**How much does it cost to visit the Xi'an City Wall?**
¥54 for adults, ¥27 for students. Bike rental is ¥45 for 2 hours (single bike). Total: about ¥100 per person.

**What is the best time to visit the Xi'an City Wall?**
Late afternoon (4:00-6:00 PM) for the best light and sunset views. The wall is less crowded on weekday mornings.

**Can you walk on the Xi'an City Wall?**
Yes, you can walk on the entire wall. It's 13.7km for the full loop, or you can walk a section (South Gate to East Gate is popular, ~3km).

**Which gate should I enter the Xi'an City Wall?**
South Gate (永宁门) is the most popular, best for sunset, and has the most facilities. For fewer crowds, try the East or North Gate.
