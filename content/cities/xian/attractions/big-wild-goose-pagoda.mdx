# 🏛️ Big Wild Goose Pagoda (大雁塔)

A 1,300-year-old Buddhist pagoda and Xi'an's most recognizable landmark — surrounded by beautiful squares and the largest musical fountain in Asia.

## Overview

The **Big Wild Goose Pagoda** (大雁塔, Dà Yàn Tǎ) was built in 652 AD during the Tang Dynasty to house Buddhist scriptures brought from India by the monk Xuanzang (the real-life inspiration for Journey to the West). It's 64 meters tall, leans slightly, and is one of China's most important Buddhist sites.

## How to Get There

- **Metro**: Line 3 or 4 to Dayanta (大雁塔站), Exit B
- **Didi/Taxi**: ~¥15-30 from city center
- **Walking**: 30 minutes south of the South Gate

## What to See

### The Pagoda
The main structure. You can climb to the top for panoramic views of Xi'an (extra ¥30). The stairs are steep and narrow — worth it for the view.

### North Square (北广场)
The largest musical fountain show in Asia:
- **Show times**: Usually 12:00 PM, 8:00 PM, 9:00 PM (check locally, varies by season)
- **Duration**: ~20 minutes
- **Cost**: Free
- **Best spot**: Stand in the center of the square facing the pagoda

### South Square (南广场)
Beautiful landscaped gardens with Tang Dynasty-style architecture. Good for photos.

### Da Ci'en Temple (大慈恩寺)
The temple complex surrounding the pagoda. Peaceful gardens, ancient halls, and incense burning.

## Pro Tips

1. **Stay for the fountain show** — it's free and genuinely impressive. The evening show (8:00 PM) is the best.

2. **Don't climb the pagoda if you have bad knees** — the stairs are steep, narrow, and there are a lot of them.

3. **Combine with the Shaanxi History Museum** — it's a 10-minute walk away and one of the best museums in China (free, book tickets in advance).

4. **The pagoda leans** — it's been tilting for centuries due to the soft ground. Engineers have stabilized it, but you can see the lean.

5. **Evening is best** — the pagoda is beautifully lit at night, and the fountain show is the highlight.

## FAQ

**How much does the Big Wild Goose Pagoda cost?**
The temple square is ¥40. Climbing the pagoda is an additional ¥30. The fountain show is free.

**What time is the Big Wild Goose Pagoda fountain show?**
Usually at 12:00 PM, 8:00 PM, and 9:00 PM. Times vary by season — check locally. The evening shows are the best.

**How long should I spend at the Big Wild Goose Pagoda?**
1-2 hours is sufficient. Add 30 minutes if you want to see the fountain show.

**Can you climb the Big Wild Goose Pagoda?**
Yes, for an additional ¥30. The stairs are steep and narrow, but the panoramic view from the top is worth it.

**Is the Big Wild Goose Pagoda worth visiting?**
Yes, especially combined with the fountain show and the nearby Shaanxi History Museum. It's one of Xi'an's most important historical sites.
