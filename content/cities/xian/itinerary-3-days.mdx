# 📅 Xi'an 3-Day Itinerary

This itinerary covers the essential Xi'an experience: Terracotta Warriors, the best street food in China, biking the ancient city wall, and a day trip to Mount Huashan.

## Day 1: Terracotta Warriors & City Wall

### Morning (7:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
⚔️ **Terracotta Warriors**
- Take Tour Bus 306 from Xi'an Train Station at 7:30 AM (¥7, ~1 hour)
- Arrive before the crowds. See all three pits + bronze chariots.
- Hire an official guide at the entrance (~¥150-200)
- See our [Terracotta Warriors Guide](/city/xian/terracotta-warriors/)

### Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:30 PM)
🏯 **Xi'an Ancient City Wall**
- Metro to South Gate (永宁门)
- Rent a bike and ride the full 14km loop (~1.5-2 hours)
- Or walk the South Gate to East Gate section (~45 min)
- See our [City Wall Guide](/city/xian/ancient-city-wall/)

### Evening (6:00 PM - 10:00 PM)
🍜 **Muslim Quarter Food Crawl**
- Walk from the Bell Tower (5 minutes)
- Eat: Roujiamo, biangbiang noodles, lamb skewers, persimmon cake
- Walk the side streets (Dapiyuan) for better food and fewer crowds
- See our [Muslim Quarter Guide](/city/xian/muslim-quarter/)

🌃 **Bell Tower at Night** — Beautiful when lit up, especially from the underground crossing

## Day 2: Culture & Deep Food Dive

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
🏛️ **Shaanxi History Museum** (陕西历史博物馆)
- One of the best museums in China — Tang Dynasty gold, terracotta figures, ancient bronzes
- **Free entry** — book tickets in advance via WeChat mini-program
- Allow 2-3 hours
- 10-minute walk from Big Wild Goose Pagoda

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
🍜 **Lunch**: Yangrou paomo (羊肉泡馍) at a dedicated restaurant near the Bell Tower
🏛️ **Big Wild Goose Pagoda** (大雁塔)
- Visit the pagoda and temple complex
- Climb to the top for city views (¥30 extra)
- See our [Big Wild Goose Pagoda Guide](/city/xian/big-wild-goose-pagoda/)
🎵 **Stay for the fountain show** (8:00 PM, free) — the largest musical fountain in Asia

### Evening (6:00 PM - late)
🍢 **Dongmutoushi (东木头市) Food Street**
- A local food street with authentic Xi'an dishes
- Try: Hulutang (gourd soup), dumplings, local noodles
- Less touristy than the Muslim Quarter

🍻 **South Gate Bar Street** — Xi'an's nightlife hub, with live music and craft beer

## Day 3: Mount Huashan Day Trip

### Full Day (6:00 AM - 9:00 PM)
⛰️ **Mount Huashan**
- Take the 7:00 AM high-speed train from Xi'an North → Huashan North (~30 min, ¥55)
- Shuttle bus to the mountain base
- West Peak cable car up → walk between peaks → North Peak cable car down
- Optional: Plank Walk (长空栈道) for the brave
- Return train ~6:00 PM, back in Xi'an by 7:30 PM

See our [Mount Huashan Guide](/city/xian/mount-huashan/) for full details.

### Alternative Day 3: Xi'an Deep Dive

If you prefer to stay in the city:

☕ **Morning**: Explore the **Stele Forest** (碑林博物馆) — ancient stone tablets and calligraphy
🛍️ **Afternoon**: **Yongxing Fang** (永兴坊) — regional Shaanxi food street
🌅 **Evening**: Walk the **South Gate wall** at sunset, then dinner at a local restaurant

## Tips

- **Book Terracotta Warriors guide in advance** — or hire at the official entrance, not from touts outside
- **Book Shaanxi History Museum tickets early** — they sell out, especially on weekends
- **Eat yangrou paomo in the morning** — it's traditionally breakfast
- **Don't skip the city wall at sunset** — it's the most photogenic time
- **Wear good shoes for Huashan** — steep stairs, uneven surfaces
- **Carry tissues** — most food stalls don't provide them
