# 🚌 Getting Around Zhangjiajie

Zhangjiajie's attractions are spread across a wide area. Here's how to navigate between the city, the National Forest Park, and the Glass Bridge.

## The Three Zones

Zhangjiajie has three main attraction zones, each requiring separate transport:

| Zone | Distance from City | How to Get There |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Tianmen Mountain** | In the city center | Walk to cable car station |
| **National Forest Park** | 30km (Wulingyuan) | Bus 40 min, taxi 30 min |
| **Glass Bridge** | 60km | Bus 1.5 hours, car 1 hour |

## Within Zhangjiajie City

### Taxi / Didi
- **Starting fare**: ¥7
- **Most trips**: ¥10-30
- **To train station**: ¥15-25 from city center

### Bus
- ¥1-2 per trip
- Routes are in Chinese only
- Useful for getting to the bus station

## Between City and Attractions

### To National Forest Park (Wulingyuan)
- **Bus**: From Zhangjiajie Bus Station (张家界汽车站), every 10 min, ¥12, ~40 min
- **Taxi**: ~¥80-120, 30 min
- **Return**: Same route, last bus around 7:00 PM

### To Glass Bridge
- **Bus**: From Zhangjiajie Bus Station, less frequent, ¥20, ~1.5 hours
- **Car hire**: ¥300-400 for a day trip (recommended)
- **No direct bus from Wulingyuan** — must go via the city

### To Tianmen Mountain
- **Walk**: The cable car station is in the city center
- **Taxi**: ¥5-15 from anywhere in the city

## Within the National Forest Park

The park has an internal shuttle bus system (included in your ticket):
- Connects all four zones (Yuanjiajie, Golden Whip Stream, Tianzi Mountain, Yangjiajie)
- Buses run frequently (every 10-15 min)
- Free with your park ticket

### Cable Cars & Elevators

| Name | Connects | Price (one-way) |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **Bailong Elevator** (百龙天梯) | Base → Yuanjiajie | ¥72 |
| **Tianzi Cable Car** (天子山索道) | Base → Tianzi Mountain | ¥72 |
| **Yangjiajie Cable Car** (杨家界索道) | Base → Yangjiajie | ¥76 |

## Recommended Itinerary Transport

### Day 1: National Forest Park
1. Bus from city → Wulingyuan (¥12, 40 min)
2. Walk to park entrance
3. Bailong Elevator up → Yuanjiajie
4. Shuttle bus between zones
5. Cable car or walk down
6. Bus back to city (or stay in Wulingyuan)

### Day 2: Tianmen Mountain
1. Walk to cable car station in city center
2. Cable car up (30 min)
3. Walk around mountaintop
4. Shuttle bus down the 99-turn road
5. Walk back to hotel

### Day 3: Glass Bridge
1. Car hire from city (¥300-400, full day)
2. Drive to Grand Canyon (1 hour)
3. Walk the bridge + canyon
4. Return to city

## Pro Tips

1. **Stay in Wulingyuan for the park** — saves 1.5 hours of commuting per day. Move to city hotel for Tianmen Mountain day.

2. **Hire a car for the Glass Bridge** — public transport is infrequent and slow. A car gives you flexibility.

3. **The park shuttle buses are free** — don't pay for a private guide who offers to "drive you around the park."

4. **Download Amap** — Google Maps doesn't work. Amap handles Zhangjiajie's winding mountain roads well.

5. **Bring motion sickness medicine** — the roads to the Glass Bridge are very winding.
