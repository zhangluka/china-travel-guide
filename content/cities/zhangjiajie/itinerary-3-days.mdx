# 📅 Zhangjiajie 3-Day Itinerary

This itinerary covers all three of Zhangjiajie's major attractions: the Avatar Mountains in the National Forest Park, Tianmen Mountain's Heaven's Gate, and the Grand Canyon Glass Bridge.

## Day 1: National Forest Park — Avatar Mountains

### Morning (7:30 AM - 12:00 PM)
🏔️ **Yuanjiajie (袁家界) — Avatar Mountains**
- Take the bus from Wulingyuan to the park entrance
- **Bailong Elevator** up to Yuanjiajie (¥72, opens 8:00 AM)
- Walk the Yuanjiajie loop trail (~2-3 hours):
  - **Avatar Hallelujah Mountain** — the iconic pillar
  - **First Bridge Under Heaven** — natural stone arch
  - **Back Garden** — hidden valley of pillars
  - **Enchanted Terrace** — panoramic viewpoint

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
🌿 **Golden Whip Stream (金鞭溪)**
- Take the shuttle bus to Golden Whip Stream entrance
- Walk the 7.5km valley trail (2-3 hours, flat and easy)
- See Golden Whip Rock, wild monkeys, and pillar-lined stream
- Exit at the south end

### Evening
🏨 **Stay in Wulingyuan Town**
- Dinner at a local restaurant — try Tujia smoked meat
- Rest early for Day 2

## Day 2: Tianmen Mountain — Heaven's Gate

### Morning (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
⛰️ **Tianmen Mountain**
- Walk to the cable car station in Zhangjiajie City (if staying in city) or take bus from Wulingyuan
- **Cable car ride** to the top (30 minutes, spectacular views)
- Walk the **cliff-hanging walkway** and **glass skywalk**
- See **Heaven's Gate** (天门洞) — the natural rock arch

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
⛩️ **Tianmen Temple** at the mountaintop
- Explore the rest of the mountaintop trails
- **Shuttle bus** down the 99-turn road
- Walk back to the city center

### Evening
🍜 **Dinner in Zhangjiajie City**
- Explore the local night market
- Rest for Day 3

## Day 3: Glass Bridge & Canyon

### Morning (7:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
🌉 **Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge**
- Hire a car from the city (arrange previous day, ~¥300-400)
- Arrive at the bridge by 8:30 AM (less crowded)
- Walk across the 430m glass bridge
- Descend into the canyon

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
🌊 **Grand Canyon Walk**
- Waterfalls, river, lush vegetation
- Optional zip line (¥50-100)
- Boat ride at the bottom
- Return to city by late afternoon

### Evening
- Depart Zhangjiajie (train or flight) or extend stay

## Tips

- **Book Glass Bridge tickets 3-5 days in advance** — daily capacity is limited
- **Check weather forecast** — Tianmen Mountain is useless in fog/cloud
- **Wear good shoes** — thousands of stairs over 3 days
- **Bring rain gear** — weather changes quickly in the mountains
- **Start early every day** — the morning light on the pillars is magical
- **Stay in Wulingyuan on Day 1** — saves commuting time for the park
