# 🐼 Chengdu — The Land of Abundance

Chengdu (成都, Chéngdū) is the capital of Sichuan province and one of China's most livable cities. Known for its laid-back lifestyle, incredible food, and giant pandas, it offers a completely different experience from Beijing or Shanghai.

## Why Visit Chengdu?

- **🐼 Giant Pandas**: The world's best panda research center is here
- **🌶️ UNESCO City of Gastronomy**: Sichuan cuisine is legendary — spicy, numbing, complex
- **🍵 Tea Culture**: Chengdu has more teahouses per capita than any other Chinese city
- **🏔️ Gateway to Western Sichuan**: Stunning mountain landscapes, Tibetan culture, and epic road trips
- **💰 Affordable**: Much cheaper than Beijing/Shanghai for food, hotels, and transport
- **😌 Relaxed Vibe**: Locals say "成都，一座来了就不想走的城市" (Chengdu — a city you won't want to leave)

## Getting There

### By Air
**Chengdu Tianfu International Airport (TFU)** — the new main airport, opened 2021.
- Direct flights from most major Asian cities
- Limited direct flights from Europe/US (usually via Beijing/Shanghai)
- Airport to city center: Metro Line 18 (~40 min, ¥10) or taxi (~¥150-200)

**Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport (CTU)** — the older airport, still in use for some domestic flights.
- Closer to city center (~20 min by metro)

### By Train
- **High-speed rail** from Beijing (8 hours), Shanghai (7 hours), Xi'an (3 hours), Chongqing (1.5 hours)
- Chengdu East Station (成都东站) is the main high-speed rail hub
- Book tickets via [Trip.com](https://www.trip.com) (English) or 12306 (Chinese)

## Getting Around Chengdu

Chengdu has an excellent **metro system** with 13+ lines covering most tourist areas. It's clean, efficient, and signs are in both Chinese and English.

- **Metro**: ¥2-8 per trip, runs 6:30am-11pm
- **Didi (ride-hailing)**: ¥15-50 for most city trips
- **Bus**: ¥2, but confusing for non-Chinese speakers
- **Taxi**: ¥8 starting fare, ¥2/km after

## What to Eat

Chengdu is a **UNESCO City of Gastronomy**. The food alone is worth the trip.

**Must-try dishes:**
- 🌶️ **Sichuan Hotpot** (火锅) — The signature experience
- 🍜 **Dan Dan Noodles** (担担面) — Spicy, nutty, addictive
- 🥟 **Zhong Dumplings** (钟水饺) — Sweet and spicy sauce
- 🫕 **Mapo Tofu** (麻婆豆腐) — Numbing spicy tofu, originated here
- 🍢 **Chuanchuan** (串串) — Skewers dipped in hotpot broth
- 🐰 **Rabbit Head** (兔头) — For the adventurous (it's actually delicious)

See our complete [Chengdu Food Guide](/city/chengdu/sichuan-food-guide/).

## Safety

Chengdu is **very safe** for foreign tourists:
- Violent crime against tourists is extremely rare
- Petty theft (pickpocketing) is uncommon but possible in crowded areas
- Scams targeting tourists are rare (unlike Beijing/Shanghai)
- The city is LGBTQ+-friendly by Chinese standards
- Air quality has improved significantly (but check AQI in winter)
